KazeTune Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

KazeTune ("we", "us", "the app") lets you pin songs to places, build playlists you can "walk", and share them with friends. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We try to collect only what the app needs to work.

Who we are

KazeTune is operated by the KazeTune team. For any privacy question or request, contact privacy@kazetune.app.

Information we collect

From your Spotify account (via Spotify login). When you sign in with Spotify, we receive your Spotify display name, email address, Spotify user ID, and profile image. We also receive access tokens that let the app control playback on your Spotify Premium account. KazeTune is only available to Spotify Premium accounts.

Content you create. Pins (including the geographic location you choose for each pin, the song and the specific clip, and any "why here" description), playlists, photos you add to pins/playlists/your profile, your chosen username and display name.

Your social connections. Friend requests and friendships, and any users you block or content you report.

Device location. With your permission, KazeTune uses your location — including in the background — to detect when you walk near a pin so it can play that pin's clip. You can grant or revoke this in iOS Settings at any time; the geofencing feature simply won't work without it.

Basic technical data. Standard logs needed to operate and secure the service.

We do not collect payment information (Spotify handles your subscription), and we do not sell your data.

How we use your information

  • To provide core features: showing pins on the map, playing clips, building and walking playlists, and connecting you with friends.
  • To control playback on your Spotify Premium account at your request.
  • To enforce safety: processing reports and blocks, and removing objectionable content or abusive users.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the service.

How your information is shared

  • Spotify. We use Spotify for authentication and playback. Your use of Spotify is also governed by Spotify's own privacy policy.
  • Supabase. We use Supabase (a hosting and database provider) to store your account and content securely on our behalf.
  • Other users. Content you mark as "friends" or "public" is visible to those audiences. Private pins are visible only to you. Your profile, username, and public/friends content can be seen by the people you've shared them with.
  • Legal. We may disclose information if required by law.

We do not otherwise share your personal information with third parties.

Your visibility controls

Every pin has a visibility setting — Private (only you), Friends (you and accepted friends), or Everyone (public). You control this per pin, and can change it at any time.

Blocking and reporting

You can block any user (which hides all content between you in both directions and removes the friendship) and report any pin, playlist, or profile. See our Terms of Use for how we handle reports.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, pins, playlists, friendships, and associated data. Deletion cannot be undone.

Children

KazeTune is not directed to children under 13, and you must be at least 13 years old to use it.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and database access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the effective date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@kazetune.app
